The numbers confirm the structure. Your dessert dynasty is genuinely tight: Lemon Zest ↔ Limonetto sits at 60% — above the 50% of a clean parent-offspring, because they re-share Lemon Butter Biscuits — Limonetto ↔ Black Limo at 53%, and Black Milk ↔ Black Limo at a clean 50%. Limonetto is 14.1% inbred and Polar Dusk 7.8% (Sunset Sherbet three times over); neither is alarming, but both are worth knowing before you breed them back into their own lines.

Three outcross islands are mathematically clean. S.A.G.E 'N Sour, Space Queen and Midnight Snow show 0% relatedness to everything — no shared documented ancestor anywhere — so each resets relatedness to zero and recovers full heterosis when crossed into the dynasty. Three others are only faintly tied in: Chilled MacMosa (~3% via MAC), Polar Dusk (~3–5% via Sunset Sherbet) and Sharpe Fuel (~4% to Polar Dusk through the Gelato / Sunset Sherbet inside its Georgia Pie side, plus a Cookies thread via Cookies S1) — outcross-ish, but not pristine.

Breeder's note — what these numbers mean for you

The inbred dessert line is a deliberate trade. A 60% Lemon Zest↔Limonetto bond and Limonetto's 14.1% inbreeding mean the line breeds true — the lemon→grapefruit character is fixed, seeds come up uniform, and you can trust the profile generation to generation. The cost is inbreeding depression: as relatedness climbs you gradually lose vigour, yield and seed-set, and you concentrate any hidden recessive faults. You're near the point where another tight cross back into the line gives diminishing returns.

Your clean outcross islands are the antidote. S.A.G.E 'N Sour, Space Queen and Midnight Snow share no documented ancestor with anything, so crossing one in restores heterosis — the hybrid-vigour rebound in yield, vigour and resilience — and injects genuinely new terpenes (Space Queen's cherry-menthol, say). The trade-off is that you also break the uniformity, so you're back to phenohunting through variable offspring to recover your signature.

Black Milk is the model for threading that needle. It's unrelated to your lemon line (0%) yet still a Cookies-family dessert, so Black Limo got a full heterosis bump and a new cherry-candy/farnesene dimension without fighting the existing flavour. That's the play: outcross to something unrelated but stylistically compatible, then select back toward your profile over a couple of generations.

Method: recursive kinship on the documented pedigree, treating the deepest recorded cultivars as unrelated founders. Deep shared Cookies/GSC ancestry below that depth isn't fully counted, so several figures — Sharpe Fuel's especially — are conservative floors and true relatedness runs a little higher. Read a literal 0 as "no documented shared ancestor," not a guarantee of none.

05

What the map says about your palette

You have one big family and a little fresh blood. The dessert side is a deliberately inbred dynasty on the Lemon Butter Biscuits backbone: Lemon Zest → Limonetto → Black Limo is a straight parent-to-grandchild line, and Sherbutter folds the same LBB into Limonetto a second time. That dynasty is your only original breeding — but your hand is on most of the library: Polar Dusk, Chilled MacMosa, S.A.G.E 'N Sour and Sharpe Fuel are keepers you pheno-hunted yourself from other breeders' seed, while Black Milk, Space Queen and Midnight Snow are clone-only cuts selected by others. Seven of ten carry either your cross or your selection. Expect high uniformity across your dynasty branch — and little vigour headroom there.

Chilled MacMosa is your sleeper bridge. Through Mac Stomper's Grape Stomper it carries Chemdawg / Sour Diesel under a dessert flavour — the one keeper that links your Cookies pole to your fuel pole without tasting like fuel.

Space Queen and Midnight Snow are your only clean outcrosses. Both share zero Cookies / Breath / OG with the dynasty. They're the obvious partners to restore hybrid vigour and add real terpene novelty — Space Queen's cherry-and-menthol on one side, old Mendo-haze purple Midnight Snow on the other — without collapsing the frame you've built.

The fuel axis is thin but real: S.A.G.E 'N Sour anchors Sour Diesel + Afghani, and Sharpe Fuel stacks Legends OG on Cookies. If you want a true gassy line, that's where to push — it's currently your least-developed direction.

Black Limo is bred from terpene monsters — and both its farnesene sources converge. Lemon Zest (5.50% terps) and Black Milk (5.93%) both hit the 100th percentile and both feed Black Limo. More specifically, Sherb Breath (4.08% terps, farnesene-led) flows in through Limonetto while Black Milk is farnesene-dominant directly — so Black Limo should come back conspicuously farnesene-forward and wash heavy for solventless.

By SC Labs' terpene classification, your library skews loud and away from the myrcene mainstream. Lemon Zest codes TXX — the textbook Jack Herer signature — landing in Jacks & Haze exactly where its Super Lemon Haze lineage predicts. S.A.G.E 'N Sour (LCX) and Chilled MacMosa (CLX) both land in OGs & Gas; for MacMosa that's the tell — the Grape Stomper Chemdawg is expressing as a gassy caryophyllene chemotype despite the dessert genetics, confirming the bridge. Black Milk reads Desserts (CLO) on the coded terpenes, but its real lead is farnesene, which SC Labs doesn't code — so it's an outlier even within its own class. None of the four are myrcene-dominant, the most common profile out there; you're selecting toward terpinolene and caryophyllene/limonene loudness.

Next steps — suggested breeding plans

Where the data points the program next, in priority order — built from the relatedness math, your terpene coverage gaps, and the cultivars you've said you enjoy.

01

Lemon Zest × Black Milk — hunt next

max heterosishighest-terp oddskeeps the lemon identity

Already in your Black Milk program, and the one I'd prioritise. Both parents hit 100th-percentile total terps (Lemon Zest 5.50%, terpinolene-led; Black Milk 5.93%, farnesene-led) and are 0% related, so you get full hybrid vigour and the best odds of a 6%+ terpene standout anywhere in the program. Unlike Black Limo, it keeps Lemon Zest at a clean 50% — the lemon/terpinolene signature stays dominant instead of being diluted through Limonetto's grapefruit detour. Run it side-by-side with Black Limo: it's the controlled test of what the Limonetto generation actually buys you. Expect phenos spanning lemon-terpinolene to cherry-farnesene, with rare ones that stack both.

02

Dolce Sativa phenohunt · Sweet Skunk × Blueberry

fills the myrcene gapBlue-Dream-adjacentfind a male donor

Your whole library skews away from myrcene — it's the one major terpene axis you don't cover, and the Sweets & Dreams class is empty. Dolce Sativa shares Blue Dream's Blueberry parent, so it should deliver the relaxing berry-myrcene profile you like. It's regs, so you've got options for a donor: keep a myrcene-dominant male, or reverse a strong female for feminized pollen. Either way you get a myrcene source to breed into your terpinolene-heavy lemon and dessert lines — something none of your current cultivars can offer. A myrcene parent over Lemon Zest, or over the dynasty, would round out that energetic citrus with weight.

03

Refresh the dynasty with an island · Space Queen or Midnight Snow × dessert line

recovers vigournew terpene axis

Limonetto is 14.1% inbred and the dessert line is tightening. Cross a 0%-related island back in to recover heterosis and add a note you don't have: Space Queen brings cherry-menthol (completely outside your palette), Midnight Snow brings old-Mendo haze. You reset relatedness to zero on the F1, then select back toward your profile — the Black Milk model applied to flavour novelty rather than potency.

04

Lean into the sour, not the gas · S.A.G.E 'N Sour

sour-citrus, not fuelpairs with your lemon lines

You've said the heavy fuel/gas funk isn't your thing — it's the sour side you want, and that plays to your strengths. S.A.G.E 'N Sour already tests limonene-led (LCX), so it leans bright sour-citrus rather than gassy caryophyllene. Select toward that limonene/sour expression and away from the fuel, and it folds naturally into your lemon program — a tangy sour-citrus accent on the HashSeed identity, not a separate gas project. It's also 0% related to everything, so it crosses in cleanly.

05
